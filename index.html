Printed posters designed during my tenure at Lidl Lietuva.
Significantly constrained by the brand's rules and guidelines in terms of creative freedom.

This project focused on redesigning the visual identity of the Šiauliai Basketball Club. The objective was to refresh the logo and color scheme, as well as enhance the overall visual identity across digital and print mediums. Please note, I am not affiliated with the club; this was a personal project undertaken to refine my design skills.
